Biography

    Dr. Abdul Jalil leads the Materials Modelling & Simulation Lab at the Department of Physics, Allama Iqbal Open University. His research focuses on computational modeling of two-dimensional materials, electronic structure calculations using Density Functional Theory (DFT), and machine learning-assisted screening for materials with photocatalytic applications.
